MEDIASTINUM — Drawable Diagram

Draw this in exam: Lateral view showing 4 compartments + contents + masses + syndromes
🧠 5 RETENTION ANCHORS:
1. T4 (sternal angle) divides superior from inferior. Everything above T4 = superior.
2. Anterior = 4 T's (Thymoma→MG 30-50%, Teratoma, Terrible lymphoma, Thyroid retrosternal)
3. Middle = pericardium + contents. TB lymphadenitis = #1 cause of middle mediastinal mass in India
4. Posterior = neurogenic tumors (schwannoma adult, neuroblastoma child). "Nerves hide in the back"
5. RHD→MS→large LA compresses: esophagus (dysphagia), L RLN (Ortner's hoarseness), L main bronchus (collapse). One disease, three compressions.
