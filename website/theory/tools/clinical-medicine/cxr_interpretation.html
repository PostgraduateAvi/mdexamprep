Silhouette Sign

Loss of a normally visible border means pathology is in the same anatomical plane as that structure.

  • Lost right heart border → RML pathology
  • Lost left heart border → Lingula pathology
  • Lost right hemidiaphragm → RLL pathology
  • Lost left hemidiaphragm → LLL pathology
